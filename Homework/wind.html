Latitude vs. Wind Speed




This plot shows relationship of latitude to wind speed. Wind speeds increase the higher in latitidue a city is. However, in lower latitudes, there are also increasing wind speeds. It should be noted that there are more northern-latitude cities in the data sample than latitudes below 0. The farther away from the equator, the faster the wind speeds.
